ABOUT US

Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering and Technology in association with the student's chapters of VCET namely IEEE,ISA,CSI,IETE,VMEA,ISHRAE,SAE,CESA,ETA organises the National level Project Showcase every year.It has been our endeavour to grow and increase our reach and span of events to enable students from all spheres to participate and compete in activities.VNPS is a National level project Showcase Event systemized by the technical committees of VCET which provides the platform for the students to display the technical abilities by putting their theoretical knowledge to practical use.
